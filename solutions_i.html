Food Grade PET Bottle Washing Line

Product Feature

"B to B" level PET bottle washing line is primarily as a first part of food grade PET bottle recycling project. Its high automation will maximally avoid human caused errors. In addition, with efficient blending and inspection systems, the quality of PET flakes ensures stable reprocessing. Our "B to B" level PET bottle washing line can configured with flexible technological process and machinery composition according to differences of actual raw material condition to meet the practical requirements of different customers. The entire production line uses industrial grade computer, large-screen HMI system and PLC to control every parameter of process directly and visually. Real-time parameter collecting and recording can be also accomplished for more convenient production management and data analysis. The equipped with automatic plastic sorting machine and color sorting machine will save manpower and improve production efficiency and thus bring actual highly automated production.

Capacity : 500 kg/h~6000kg/h
